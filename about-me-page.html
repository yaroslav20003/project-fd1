hello

I am a digital artist creating her work entirely on a computer using a graphics tablet.One day I decided to combine two of my favorite hobbies - reading and drawing, and now I am creating custom art from books.

It all started with the fact that in one of the social networks I began to tell my impressions about the books I read. That's how my audience grew. In parallel with this time, I finally managed to get my first graphics tablet. As my hand got used to the equipment, I thought, “Why not draw a character from a book?” After all, now more and more stores are gaining popularity, where you can buy not only books, but also the most diverse merchandise on them.

history

At first I drew for myself, and later I was noticed by the owners of stores specializing in book paraphernalia. This is how my (career???) was born.At the moment, my art is completely used by the authors of books for paper cards, bookmarks, acrylic figurines and stands, candles, and even huge canvases. Most of them you can see in my Gallery.

There you will find art based on books by the famous Sarah J. Maas from her Kingdom of Thorns and Roses series, as well as books by popular authors such as Jennifer Armentrout, Holly Black, Ali Hazelwood, Mara Wolfe, Leah Arden, Shelby Maherin and many more other.
